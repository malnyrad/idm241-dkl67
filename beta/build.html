Triggers

Icon Sidebar (Memo)

  • Hover On
  • Hover Off
  • On Click (Mouse Up)

Rules

Memo (Default)

  • Hover On
    • Cursor changes to finger.
    • Square area around icon quickly fades from white to light gray.
    • "Memo" tagline appears to the left of the square area.
  • Hover Off
    • Cursor changes back to pointer.
    • Square area around icon quickly fades back to white.
    • "Memo" tagline disappears.
  • On Click (Mouse Up)
    • Side panel slides to the left from the right side of the screen (offscreen).

Memo (Selected)

  • Hover On

  • Hover Off

  • On Click (Mouse Up)
    • Side panel slides to right (offscreen) from the left.
